Mental Health First Aid (MNFA) is a training program that teaches the public how to help a person developing a mental health problem, experiencing a worsening of an existing mental health problem or in a mental health crisis through courses.

Mental Health America (MHA) is a nonprofit organization that addresses the needs of those living with mental illness and promotes overall mental health. They also provide prevention services, early identification and intervention, and integrated care, services, and support for those who need it.

The National Alliance on Mental Illness (NAMI) is an advocacy group that provides education, support and public awareness so that all individuals and families affected by mental illness can build better lives. They also have a helpline (NAMI Helpline) in which they respond personally to and provide free information and support to hundreds of thousands of requests each year.

Child Mind institute is an independent non-profit organization that provides advice and support to children struggling with mental health and learning disorders They also campaign to improve services, raise awareness, promote understanding and empower anyone experiencing a mental health problem.

The Better Health Channel provides health and medical information to help people understand and manage their health and medical conditions, and help them make lifestyle choices based on their own personal needs.
