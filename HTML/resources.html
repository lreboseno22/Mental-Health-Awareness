Mental Health America

Mental Health America (MHA) is a nonprofit organization that addresses the needs of those living with mental illness and promotes overall mental health. They also provide prevention services, early identification and intervention, and integrated care, services, and support for those who need it.

National Alliance on Mental Ilness

The National Alliance on Mental Illness (NAMI) is an advocacy group that provides education, support and public awareness so that all individuals and families affected by mental illness can build better lives. They also have a helpline (NAMIHelpline) in which they respond personally to and provide free information.

The Child Mind

The Child Mind Institute is an independent non-profit organization that provides advice and support to children struggling with mental health and learning disorders. They also campaign to improve services, raise awareness, promote understandingand empower anyone experiencing a mental health problem.

Mental Health First Aid

Mental Health First Aid (MNFA) is a training program that teaches the public how to help a person developing a mental health problem, experiencing a worsening of an existing mental health problem or in a mental health crisis through courses.
